About Fon

Fon is your WiFi network

Fon's mission is to blanket the world with WiFi, so you can easily upload pictures, stream music and videos and update your social network profiles from anywhere. Whether you're relaxing in your living room, having a tea at your local coffee shop or shopping in Tokyo, Fon is where you are going.

How does Fon work?

Fon splits your WiFi signal into two. A private one that is just for you, and a public one, which you share with other Fon members. Both networks are secure and since the public signal is created from the unused portion of your bandwidth, you will still be able to enjoy the same connection speeds as before. Additionally, you will not be held liable for the use of your public network.

As a sharing Fon member, you will be able to enjoy all of the network's hotspots both in Belgium and abroad for free.

Not ready to become a member? We make it easy and affordable for you to connect to the network. Just buy a pass and connect to any of our hotspots!

Want to learn more?

Visit it us at fon.com or talk to one of the millions of Fon members worldwide active on all the major social networks.

About Proximus

Proximus: partner of Fon in Belgium

As a Proximus Internet customer you will receive unlimited free access to Fon's huge global Wi-Fi network, as well as all Proximus Wi-Fi hotspots in partnership with Fon in Belgium. Enjoy fast Internet speeds on all your Wi-Fi-enabled devices. Stream music, watch videos and play games, just like at home. All you need to do is connect to a Wi-Fi hotspot in Belgium or any Fon hotspot in the world and you are ready to go online!

Because I want to surf when I'm not at home too

Replying to an e-mail in a café? Checking Facebook on holidays? Reading the newspaper online while waiting for the bus? With Proximus Wi-Fi hotspots in partnership with Fon you can surf anywhere for free on your laptop, tablet or smartphone. As a Proximus Internet customer, you get access to millions of Wi-Fi hotspots in Belgium and abroad (UK, France, Portugal …).

More Internet without paying more

Anyone with a Proximus Internet subscription can use the Wi-Fi hotspots free of charge. The data volume you use via the Wi-Fi hotspots will simply be deducted from your monthly – often unlimited – usage. So it's like surfing at home.

I choose the best quality

The Wi-Fi hotspots offer the same quality as at home. Web pages load as quick, you can watch videos online in the same quality and you listen just as easily to music.
